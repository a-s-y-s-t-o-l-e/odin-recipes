Buddy the Elfs Spagetti

Using his superior intellect combined with his exquisite pallet, Chef Buddy has created one of the greatest dishes known to man. This recipe is sure to be the life of the Christmas party. Your kids will love you and your stomachs will hate you.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 pound uncooked spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup pure maple syrup, plus more for topping

Toppings

  • 1/3 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1/3 cup M&M's
  • 1/3 cup candy corn
  • 1/4 cup sprinkles
  • 1/4 cup jelly beans
  • chocolate syrup

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of water with a pinch of salt to boil using high heat.
  2. Add 1 pound of uncooked spaghetti and cook unil al dente, about 10 minutes.
  3. Drain well and toss with 2 tablespoons unsalted butter and 1/3 cup of maple syrup
  4. Add toppings and drizzle with maple syrup
  5. Enjoy and regret decisions that brought you to this
